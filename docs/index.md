## About KaryoScope

KaryoScope is a graph-based method for analyzing long-read sequencing data that identifies structural patterns in sequences based on genomic feature composition and transition patterns. The analysis pipeline uses hierarchical clustering to group sequences by their structural similarity, enabling discovery of sample-specific structural variants and telomeric arrangements.
